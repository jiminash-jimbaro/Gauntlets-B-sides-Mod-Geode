# Gauntlet B Sides

a mod that adds custom b sides to some gauntlets 
filled with 5 levels fitting the theme of the original gauntlet but "harder" than the original gauntlet 


## Current gauntlets added
- fire gauntlet
- ice gauntlet
- poison gauntlet
- shadow gauntlet
- bonus gauntlet
